Welcome to my homepage.

I am an astrophysicist working in the field of planet formation and evolution, both from a theoretical perspective and by observing exoplanet systems with state-of-the-art telescopes.

Since September 2017, I am a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy in Heidelberg and a fellow of the IMPRS School for Astronomy and Cosmic Physics.

My PhD focusses on population synthesis of exoplanets around M stars.
