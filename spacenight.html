As an astronomer, I feel fortunate to explore the wonders of our Universe in a society that values and supports scientific discovery. I am passionate about sharing the latest discoveries in exoplanet science and astronomy, which inspired me to found Space Night Augsburg. This science communication and fundraiser event series directly connects astronomers with the public while helping to bring next-generation science education into classrooms.
